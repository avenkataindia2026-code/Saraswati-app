Privacy by Design

  • Zero Data Collection: We do not collect, share, or store any personal information.
  • Local Analysis: Your voice is processed in real-time on your device. Audio is never recorded or uploaded.
  • No Tracking: No analytics, no cookies, no third-party SDKs.
  • Minimal Permissions: Microphone access is used exclusively for frequency detection.
